Sheth Anandilal Podar(School established in 1927)

Podar Education Network is a network of schools in India, managed by the Anandilal Podar Trust of Mumbai. The trust was established in 1927 by a Mumbai-based businessman Sheth Anandilal Podar. As of December 2014, the group had 116 schools across India.
